Lili

One evening, I received a message from his friend asking if I had a boyfriend. I said no, and he told me he had a friend who liked seeing me. So I gave him my number, and soon after, his friend messaged me. We started talking and getting to know each other. The following Monday, we talked in person for the first time, and I was so shy. When we were saying goodbye, he hugged me; I was surprised because I didn’t expect it. Then he asked me out on a mini lunch date on Tuesday, August 19th. A few days later, on August 22nd, he asked me to be his girlfriend. Of course, I said yes; I liked him too. And that’s how our love story began… and it’s still continuing. ❤️
